# Smithy

The Smithy is the camp’s primary martial workshop, responsible for maintaining, improving, and forging weapons and armor.  
All smithing actions are performed by interacting directly with **Kaelen Rook** while camp is active.

The Smithy progresses through **five levels**, each unlocking stronger materials, upgrades, and crafting options.

> **Important:**  
> Smithy upgrades are limited by the current level of the **Builder’s Table**.

---

## Smithy Operator

**Name:** Kaelen Rook  
**Race:** Human  
**Age:** 24  
**Build:** Lean, muscular  

---

## Smithy Progression Overview

| Level | Capability |
|------|-----------|
| I | Basic Crafting & Repairs |
| II | Reinforcement & Ammunition |
| III | +1 Enchantments |
| IV | Masterwork & +2 Gear |
| V | Legendary Foundations |

# Level I — Basic Smithy

### Requirements
- Builder’s Table Level I
- Iron forge
- Basic tools

### Functions
- Repair weapons and armor
- Craft common weapons and armor

---

## Smithy Rules & Notes

- Items must be **physically present**
- Upgrades consume **time + materials**
- Smithy cannot exceed Builder’s Table level
- Kaelen may refuse reckless or cursed work

---

## DM Design Notes

- Legendary crafting should feel *earned*
- Materials can gate narrative regions
- Kaelen’s loyalty shapes what risks he accepts
